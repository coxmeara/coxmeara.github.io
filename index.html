Women in Computer Science

Statistics About Women in Computer Science

Only 20% of computer science degrees earned in 2021 were by women.

Computer Science has one of the lowest pay gaps between men and women, with women earning 94% of what men earn.

Only about 20% of computer scientist are women.

In 2020, companies in the Forbes Fortune 500 had a 66% increase in ROI if they had at least three women in leadership roles.

In secondary school, less emphasis is placed on developing computer science skills in girls compared to boys.

Reasons Why More Women Shoud Get Into Computer Science

Many tech companies offer flexible hours, allowing employees to work around their personal schedules to complete projects.

Tech companies need more women programmers because studies show diverse teams are more innovative.

Through computer science women can make positive changes in the world.

Women tend to pay more attention to detail which can help them greatly in their ability to program.

Research has found women working in tech companies -- particularly start-ups -- are much more likely to have a healthy work-life balance.
